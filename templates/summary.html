Gender ratio in the dataset
The very famous "Adult Income Dataset" is used here to debias gender biases. This dataset named “adult” is found in the UCI machine learning repository. It consists of 16 columns of which the targetted column is Income which is divded into 2 catagories ">50K" and "<=50K". Other attributes are demographics and features that describe a person such as age, gender, occupation, race, relationship..etc.
